The tutorials build on each other. The simple site comes first; from there you fill it in with assets, images, and head extras, then publish it through sitemaps, multilingual, and deployment. Debugging comes last, as the tool you reach for when something is off.

New to Baseline? The [overview](/docs/introduction/overview/) covers what it is and who it is for.
